Our Story

A legacy of fresh seafood and Puerto Rican hospitality spanning over seven decades

1949

The Beginning

Ladi's Place opens its doors in Playa de Salinas, becoming Puerto Rico's first seafood restaurant. Local fishermen bring their daily catch directly to our kitchen, establishing a tradition that continues today.

1950s

Birth of Mojo Isle√±o

Our founder creates the legendary Mojo Isle√±o, a signature Puerto Rican seafood sauce that becomes famous throughout the island. This recipe remains our most treasured secret.

2010s

Expansion to San Juan

We open our second location in Puerta de Tierra, San Juan, bringing our authentic seafood experience to the capital with breathtaking Caribbean Sea views.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

Three generations later, we continue to serve the freshest seafood in Puerto Rico, maintaining the same dedication to quality and authenticity that started it all.

Our Philosophy

At Ladi's Place, we believe in the simple beauty of fresh seafood prepared with love and respect for tradition. Every morning, local fishermen bring us the finest catch from Puerto Rico's coastal waters. Every dish honors our heritage while celebrating the incredible bounty of the Caribbean Sea.

"From our shores to your table, with pride and passion."
